----Brief history about the Church and Pastor----



The church "Christ On Mount Carmel Assembly" was established on the 17th of December 2016 by pastor J.O oluyele.

Through revelation the name "Christ On Mount Carmel Assembly" was given to the man of God.

We then started fellowships and prayer meetings and finally the church was established.

We also started envangelism immediately. We believe in high praise, evangelism.




OUR GOALS AND OBJECTIVES


As it is written in Mark 16:15-19, Matt.28:19-20, Lk. 4:13-19, Jer 1:5;10 and 18


We are commissioned by the Lord Jesus Christ for the following;


(a) To teach the full gospel; Of salvation, healing, deliverance and baptism of the Holy Ghost,
to reach to sinners and bring them back to the image and likeness of God through Jesus Christ our Lord.



(b) To teach Saints; Within the Churches of God in which we and other members of the body of Christ
are gathered as the spirit of God leads us for the purposes started in Act. 15:36, Act 16: 1-15.



(c) To plant "CHURCH OF GOD"; By gathering converts in location of home or places of worship like: Tempeles
so erected for fellowship or prayer mettings, bible study, teaching of doctrine of Jesus Christ,
the Apostles breaking of bread known as The Lord's Supper.
